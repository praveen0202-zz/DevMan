About us

Welcome to DevManoj Enterprises

We are based in Channapattana ( located in Ramnagar District, Karnataka) which has a reputation world wide for its wooden toys & wooden crafted gift items. The main intension of starting this firm is to taking forward our historical values & works of channapattana toys and wooden items to our future generation and also to protect the livelihood of our artisans who are engaged in this art work. Apart from our historical wooden toys and crafted items, we also manufacture best quality wooden furnitures.
